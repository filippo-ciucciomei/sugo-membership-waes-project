Sugo is a London-based cycling club founded in 2021 by a group of riders who couldn’t quite find the right fit in existing clubs. We built the club around a shared mindset: having fun, riding fast, eating carbs.
We ride every week in Regent’s Park on Tuesdays and Fridays at 7:00am (weather permitting — dry roads and above 0°C). On most weekends we organise longer rides outside the city.
You’re welcome to join us for a weekday Regent’s Park ride to get a feel for the group. If you enjoy riding with us, you can purchase a club membership to access all our weekend rides and the full Sugo calendar.

We look forward to seeing you out on the road.
